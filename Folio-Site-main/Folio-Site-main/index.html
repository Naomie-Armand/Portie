My Background / About Me
With a background in Tech Support and a passion for design, I care deeply about making technology more intuitive and inclusive. I’ve spent years helping people troubleshoot confusing systems, which showed me how important thoughtful, human-centered design really is. That experience shaped the way I build, always with the end user in mind.
Whether I’m sketching ideas, fixing bugs, or building something from scratch, I focus on real people and real problems. I’m especially interested in accessibility, clean UI, and simplifying workflows. I believe the best solutions don’t just work; they make life easier. I'm always learning, experimenting, and looking for ways to build tools that are not just functional but meaningful.
